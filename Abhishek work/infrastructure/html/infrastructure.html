We strive to learn beyond mere facts and statistics to acquire forms that will definitely be an aid to us in life. The library acts as a great stimulating agent, offering articles, data, and anecdotes on topics as diverse as possible. It is an absolute paradise for book-lovers. From important sample papers of the BOARD EXAMS to delightful one-Act plays, browsing through books in any corner of the library is an experience, both enlightening and pleasurable. Managed exceptionally well by Mrs. Reena Verma, the ambience of the library has an innate ability to motivate youngsters to learn and broaden their perspective. Equipped with around 32,000 books, the library caters to every subject, be it history, social studies, science, literature, psychology, sociology or economics. In many ways the library has made us realize where our interest and skills lie. It has encouraged us to dream and work tirelessly and has taught us that learning is not just limited to mechanical classroom teaching methods but it can also be real fun.
