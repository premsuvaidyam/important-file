CHAPEL

A SANCTUARY FOR PRAYER Peace, perhaps, is more about our state of mind and less about the scheme of things around us. In a world that has come to endorse quick thinking and doing, the Chapel encourages us to sit, analyse, and reflect on our actions. The Sisters have taught us to place great emphasis on prayer, thanking God for the divine plan that He has made for each one of us. Over the years, we Notre Damians have learnt to establish a deep bond with the Lord that permeates our daily life and our contact with others, providing us with the required energy and enthusiasm to go about life, with all its obstacles and challenges. The Chapel encourages us to leave our worries behind, engage ourselves in communication with God, and try to imbibe the goodness and selflessness that is truly Notre Damian. The students frequent the chapel for everything, from ailing pets to desirable marks in upcoming tests. The Chapel resonates with energy and warmth, making a visit to it absolutely mandatory for a fruitful day.
