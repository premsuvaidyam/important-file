Small Auditorium

The two integral pillars of the Notre Dame haven are the Small Auditorium and the Julie Hall. Initially, the Small Auditorium started off as a pedestal where the students could showcase their teeming talents to the entire Notre Dame family. Gradually, as the strength of the school grew, it became a centre for meetings, cultural and co-curricular activities, Visual and Performing Arts, Work Education and Science exhibitions to name a few. Over the years, as the school became more and more populous, an urgent need was felt for the creation of a new performing arena, and thus, the magnificent Julie Hall came into being. It is one such platform, designed for the purpose of cultivating finesse in the students.

Notre Dame, in all its glory is incomplete and barren without the splendor of these intrinsic parts of the school.
