Language Lab

AN ABODE OF LEARNING LANGUAGE

Good communication skills are indispensable for the success of any professional. If one wants to reach out to people, she has to speak their language. The English language, in particular, has become essential in the lives of young people who aspire to advance their careers anywhere in the world. Notre Dame hence has introduced the Language lab in its glorious architecture. As it is a technological aid for learning, it has a number of advanced facilities that can help a student to learn a language with proficiency. Equipped with computers, the language lab enables the students to enhance their oratory skills by audio and visual technologies. They improve their diction, their accent, and enhance their grammatical knowledge. The language laboratory is playing an important role in the language learning process. It has encouraged students to speak mellifluously in public. It has taught us that, language is not only learnt for exams but for its practical use in day to day life.
