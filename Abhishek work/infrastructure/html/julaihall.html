HALL FOR BUDDING ARTISTS

For the transition that every Notre Damian makes from innocence to poise, she has to come across the inevitable hurdles of insecurity, disappointment, and low self esteem. It is, indeed, a long fruitful journey for all of us, showering us with opportunities to overcome our fears, hone our talents, and make our creative abilities visible.

Julie Hall is one such platform, designed and conceived for the very purpose of growth and refinement. From the colour and cheer of the fancy dress competitions in the Montessori to the serenity of the Graduation Ceremony. The walls and stage have seen our girls grow up and learn to face the world.

The present Julie Hall was officially inaugurated on November 11, 1989, with a prayer service and a blessing given by Father James Cox, SJ. A musical relating the story of Ali Baba and the Forty Thieves with an ingenious slant was held on November 12th and 13th, 1989. Now it is a fully air conditioned hall . All the cultural activities of the school right from the Montessori Concert, the One Act Play and Graduation Ceremony to the Teachers’ day celebrations, debates and spelling contests take place in the Julie Hall.

Julie Hall has also looked fondly upon students while they recited their first nursery rhymes on stage for the Montessori concerts and as they walked down the aisle in sophisticated white sarees to receive our diplomas. Memories of some enlightening Career Counselling sessions and amazing Teachers’ Day and Appreciation Days, when our claps and cheers reverberated through the hall, are enough to realize that it hasn’t just been a stage but a source of shelter, solace, and inspiration. Innumerable visits to the Hall instil in students a desire to be winners in every walk of life, teach them to value and respect themselves and be survivors – for defeat is only an experience, not an eventuality.

Julie Hall stands for all that NDA is and all that every young girl entering its portals is expected to imbibe - confidence, fortitude, humility, generosity, and optimism.
