Maths Lab

TRAINING YOUNG MATHEMATICIANS

The school is gifted with an exceptional Maths Lab which has been designed for the practical implications of the theory that we learn. The Lab is centrally air conditioned. It is equipped with nine sets of computers connected to a centralized server, which have interactive modules for the better understanding of the concepts. It has various teaching aids and provides an audio-visual interface to the students.
