School's Management

R.N. PUBLIC School is established and administered by Patna Notre Dame Sisters
Society. Under the Fundamental Rights guaranteed by Article 30 of the Indian Constitution , for
students professing christian faith, but it also admits others irrespective
of caste and creed.

R.N.P.S. Sisters Society has final authority over and responsibility for R.N.P.S.

Committee of Management:

The authority to manage and conduct all affairs of the Institution may be vested in a committee which shall be responsible for properly running the Institution in accordance
with the general policy laid down by the society as decided in its meetings
from time to time and the regulations and code, if any, prescribed for its institutions.

The committee shall consist of the following:

  • President
 - President of the Patna Notre Dame Sisters Society Ex-Officio
  • Vice- President
 - The Principal of Notre Dame Academy, Ex-Officio
  • Secretary
 - A member of the Society appointed by thePresident of Patna Notre Dame Sisters Society Ex-officio
  • Members

-Four members of said society nominated by the President of Society.

-Two respresentatives of the staff appointed by the Principal.

-A guardians’ respresentative nominated by the rest of the members of the committee of management for a term of three years.

-One Gazatted Govt.official appointed by the President for a period of 3 years.

-An Educationist appointed by the Management Committee for a period of 3 years.
